About Naawi-Oodena

Who We Are

Naawi-Oodena means "centre of the heart and community" in Anishinaabemowin. This area used to be a military base, but now it's being transformed into a place for Indigenous families and future generations to live, work, and thrive.

Our Vision

Our goal is to build a sustainable and inclusive urban reserve that celebrates Indigenous culture, creates opportunities, and connects people through strong community planning.
