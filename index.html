NAAWI OODENA

Centre of the Heart and Community

Naawi-Oodena" is an Anishinaabe word which means "centre of the heart and community.It is a 160-acre Indigenous urban development on the former Kapyong Barracks site. A partnership between Treaty One Nation that has (68% of the land) and Canada Lands Company which has(32% of the land), making it the largest urban reserve in Canada.

Housing

Single-family homes and multi-unit buildings blending modern design with cultural values.

Cultural Spaces

Community gathering spaces, a cultural centre, and public plazas honoring Indigenous traditions.
